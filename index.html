Welcome to the OFFICIAL FANSITE of Starrpark

Dear visitor,

We spent the last years collecting ephemera and images from the notoriously defunct theme park STARR PARK, mainly from the 60's and early 70's, with some exceptions from the late 50's during the conceptualization phase of the park. We are very proud to share these works with you, divided into two categories, entitled GALLERIES, and INVESTOR VIDEO

We hope you can enjoy them as much as we enjoyed looking for them.
